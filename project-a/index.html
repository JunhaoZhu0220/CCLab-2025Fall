[ MAGNEMITE ]

Scientific Name:

Magnemite

Discovered by / at:

Doctor Zhu, 2033

Family:

Electrovitae

Habitat:

Discovered in energy-rich Dark-Energy Voids. They exhibit stable life signs within a laboratory environment and appear most active against a dark environment.

Appearance:

This creature exists in two distinct forms:

  • Singularis Form: Exists as two independent individuals, known as the "Positive" and the "Negative". Each individual is a spherical body with a large, single eye.
  • The two individuals lock together, forming a stable bipolar structure. In this form, their center emits a energy aura. The aura shifts from a dim glow to an intense magenta. This aura can also generate thunder...

Key Characteristics:

  • Attract each other like a magnet.
  • Like to absorb yellow charges.
  • Extremely dangerous when charging.
  • Seem to be sensitive to red charges.
  • Its power may decay.

What we know so far about [ Magnemite ]?
The discovery of Magemite was purely accidental. Doctor Zhu first recorded this peculiar energy-based lifeform in 2033 during the 'VoidSim' project. Initially, only single individuals (either red or blue) were generated in the simulator. They appeared lifeless.
The breakthrough occurred during a dual-instantiation test: When a 'Positive' and a 'Negative' individual were introduced into the simulation simultaneously, they immediately displayed a strong mutual attraction and quickly combined into a stable, humming form.
Further observations revealed that different types of charges may trigger distinct behaviors.
